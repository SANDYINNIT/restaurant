How to Find Us

We’re located at Avinguda Catalunya, 100, Badalona, Spain. Use the map below or follow the written directions.

By Car

  1. Take Highway A toward City Center.
  2. Exit at Main Street and turn right.
  3. Continue for 500m; we’ll be on your left.

By Public Transport

  • Bus 22: Stop at Main St. & 1st Ave.
  • Walk 200m north to the restaurant entrance.
